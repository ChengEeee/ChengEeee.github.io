I am a Master student in Artificial intelligence at Tsinghua University, advised by Houde Liu and Linqi Ye. Previously, I completed my Bachelor's in Mechanical design and automation at XiDian University, advised by Jiaji Wu.

My research area include Legged/Humanoid Robot, Embodied AI. My interests lie in the Embedded Systems Design of ARM/Linux/Risc-V Based. I care about and continue to think about robots and embodied intelligence in real application scenarios to achieve commercial closed loop.

Robots might not be ready to take over the world, but they're on their way to mastering the coffee run. If you're interested in diving deeper into my research or swapping entrepreneurial insights, let's connect!

Quadruped Robot Hardware Capable of Multi-Terrain Locomotion
Yi Cheng, Xinwei Zhao, Yukai Shi, Jing Li, Ziyang Jiao, Bo Zhang, Zhengwei Li
We built a quadruped robot with upper-mounted motors and timing belt-driven legs, using aluminum and 3D-printed parts. It features MPC+WBC for motion control and uses Intel's D435i and RoboSense's 32-line LiDAR for perception.
